Home Watch vs. Home Sitter

Home Watch Services:

Home Watch companies offer comprehensive oversight to safeguard your home while you're away. Our diligent professionals conduct thorough inspections to detect potential issues such as water leaks, mildew, pest infestation, and unlocked windows. With our expertise, we provide detailed inspection reports, ensuring you have peace of mind knowing your property is well-maintained and secure.

Home Sitter Services:

A Home Sitter may provide temporary occupancy of your residence, offering a sense of security with their presence. However, their focus is often on personal comfort rather than home maintenance. While they may sleep in your home and watch television, they typically do not provide inspection reports or address potential issues proactively.

Choose Home Watch for Peace of Mind

When it comes to protecting your home, opt for a Home Watch service that prioritizes thorough inspections and proactive maintenance. With Home Watch, you receive comprehensive care and detailed reports, ensuring your property remains in optimal condition even in your absence.
